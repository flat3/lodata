# Requirements

Lodata v5 works with:

  • Laravel 8 (PHP 7.3 - 8.1)
  • Laravel 9 (PHP 8.0 - 8.2)
  • Laravel 10 (PHP 8.1 - 8.3)

To use any of the PDO database connectors you must have the correct PHP extensions installed.
